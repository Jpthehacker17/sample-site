What is RMDME?

RMDME is a reminder app that helps you remember things in a more fun and engaging way. It uses the power of AI to create personalized reminders that are tailored to your needs and preferences. Whether you need to remember to take your medication, pay your bills, or call your mom, RMDME can help you stay on track and never forget again.
